Harvest haven

build, create, and explore!

HOW TO PLAY

Use WASD to move your character around, walking up to a stone or tree and clicking the object allows you to gather. Clicking the different options to your right allows you to craft and place said item. The cost is also stated after the name of the item.

OBJECTIVE

You have been entrusted with fixing up the farm that your now deceased grandfather has left behind. Create a farm that you are satisfied with from planting your own crops and designing your farm with different pathways to upgrading your house. We hope you have fun and enjoy this stress free game.

DESCRIPTION

Our project, Harvest Haven, which is inspired by games like Minecraft and Stardew Valley is a procedurally generated game where users are able to explore our world and design their world with items like houses, fences, plants, and pathways.

this game was created by Edwin Fong, Patrick Hu, Jackie Huang, Yingting Huang, Brady Lin, and Frank Shi.
