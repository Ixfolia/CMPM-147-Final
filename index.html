Harvest haven

build, create, and explore!

HOW TO PLAY

Use WASD to move your character around, walking up to a stone or tree and clicking the object allows you to gather. With the drop down underneath the game, you are able to click what you want to craft and place. Completion is based off of your amount of materials, nothing will happen if you do not have enough wood and/or stone.

DESCRIPTION

Our project, Harvest Haven, which is inspired by games like Minecraft and Stardew Valley is a procedurally generated game where users are able to explore our world and design their world with items like houses, fences, plants, and pathways. We hope you have fun!
